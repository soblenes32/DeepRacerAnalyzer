Navigate to AWS IAM

Click here to go to AWS IAM. Sign in to your AWS account if prompted. Click on the "users" button in the IAM left-hand navigation menu.

 Create an IAM user

Click on the blue "add user" button at the top of the users view.

 Name the new IAM user

Choose a name for the user that will have permission to download DeepRacer logstreams. Check the "programmatic access" checkbox. Click on "Next: permissions".

 Navigate to group selection

Click on the "create group" button.

 Select group permissions

Enter a group name and check "CloudWatchReadOnlyAccess", "AmazonS3ReadOnlyAccess". Submit the form.

 Complete IAM user creation

Click "Next: Tags". Click "Next: Review". Click "Create user". Download your user's credentials and save them to a safe place.

 Navigate to your training logs

Navigate to the AWS CloudWatch management console by clicking here. Click on "Log groups" in the side nav, and then click on "/aws/sagemaker/TrainingJobs"

Copy log stream name

Select a log stream from the table. Identify the stream that corresponds to your training session of interest by looking at the timestamp embedded in the stream name (highlighted). Copy the entire stream name.
